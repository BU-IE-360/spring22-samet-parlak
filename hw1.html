CONCLUSION¶

All of the graphs have anomalies around the time "ISIS terror" keyword begins trending. This idea can also be supported by observing the COVID anomalies. Despite the fact the early COVID era shows much declines in every graph, the keyword trending era cannot be overlooked since these eras are the only sudden declines in every graph. The analysis shows that tourism industry the most affected by terror because it has the record low excluding the early COVID era.
